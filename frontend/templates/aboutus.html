About Us:

Our Mission:

Blue Rhino is a web app that offers an innovative new way for individuals to create and share their business cards in a completely virtual way.

Our organization's long-term goal is to create a fully functional web app for either the eco-conscious consumer or business professional through the use of online business cards. In order to accomplish this we will create a seamless process to create user accounts, connect email and cell to our platform, and to easily connect with others with our platform.
