Why E-Business Cards?

On average each year 8 million trees are cut down just for business cards. That is 10 billion business cards printer each year. 88% of these cards are either thrown away, misplaced, or destroyed. That means 8.8 billion worth of trees are wasted each year. We create all these GoGreen programs but cannot stop wasting 7 million trees?

    Helping the Enviroment?

  • Eliminating paper wastage.
  • Updating and Sychronizing Business Cards.
  • Accesible to multiple platforms ranging from educational to professional expertise .
